About Us

Genezyme Solutions is a company operating in the biotechnology and life sciences domain, specializing in innovative solutions for research and industrial applications.
Its focus areas include the development and supply of advanced laboratory equipment, tools, and services to support cutting-edge research in molecular biology, genomics,
and related fields.


Key Offerings:

Products:

Electrophoresis Systems: Used for DNA, RNA, and protein analysis.

Power Packs: Precision instruments for electrophoresis and lab experiments.

Pipetting Robots: Automating liquid handling tasks for high accuracy.

Molecular Biology Equipment: Specialized tools for various biological research needs.


Services:
Customization of equipment based on research needs.
Technical support and maintenance services.
Consultation for setting up biotech labs.


Industries Served:
Academic and research institutions.
Pharmaceutical and biotech companies.
Diagnostic labs and clinical research organizations.


Mission:
To empower scientists and researchers with reliable, innovative tools that simplify
complex processes and accelerate discoveries in life sciences.
